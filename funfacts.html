Fun Facts

  • Both of his parents worked on the first computer, you could say it runs in the family!
  • The first website he created, still online today, was dedicated to explaining the World Wide Web itself.
  • He once described himself as a "tech hippie," believing in the free flow of information without corporate control.
  • He enjoys sailing and once said that the freedom of the open sea reminded him of the open nature of the web.
